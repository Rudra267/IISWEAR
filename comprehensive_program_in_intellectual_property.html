Programme Overview

This Impactful IPR Program is designed to produce job-ready professionals in Intellectual Property (IP) law and strategy by offering in-depth training across key IP domains: patents, trademarks, copyrights, geographical indications, and plant variety protections etc. Through actual client patent projects, and global perspectives, learners acquire the right skills and legal insight required to thrive in the evolving field of intellectual property.

Programme Modules

Module 0: Introduction to the World of Intellectual Property Rights and Patents

  • Overview of Utility, Process & Design Patents, Trademark, Copyright, Geographical Indication (GI) etc.
  • Outline of Approving Authorities including Microbial Type Culture Collection and Gene Bank (MTCC), National Biodiversity Authority (NBA), World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO).
  • Summary of Relevant Treaties of IPR including Key International IPR Treaties like Patent Cooperation Treaty (1970), Berne Convention (1886) for copyright, Madrid Protocol for trademarks, Budapest Treaty (1977) for microorganisms, and the TRIPS Agreement (1995) for comprehensive IPR standards.

Module 1: Mastering Trademarks: Law, Strategy & Protection

  • Fundamental Trademark Concepts & Applicable Legal Sections
  • Procedural Framework & Filing Guidelines of Trademark
  • Essential Forms & Compliance Documentation for Trademark
  • Effective Use of Trademark Database & Search Strategies including AI Search
  • Trademark Clearance Search Documentation & Recommendation
  • Replying to Trademark Objection
  • Trademark Assignment to Interested Parties

Module 2: Copyright: Law, Rights & Enforcement

  • Fundamental Copyright Concepts & Legal Framework
  • Procedural Aspects & Registration
  • Essential Forms & Compliance Documentation for Copyright
  • Effective Use of Copyright Database & Search Strategies including AI Search
  • Digital Copyright & Emerging Challenges
  • Enforcement & Infringement Protection
  • Licensing & Assignment Strategies

Module 3: Design Law Mastery: Registration, Rights & Remedies including Semiconductor Design Law

  • Fundamental Concepts of Industrial Designs & Legal Framework
  • Design Search & Prior Art Analysis
  • Preparation & Filing of Design Applications
  • Examination Process & Responding to Objections
  • Registration, Publication & Rights Conferred
  • Design Protection Duration, Renewal & Lapse

Module 4: Mastering Patents: Law, Innovation & Protection

  • Fundamental Patent Concepts & Legal Framework
  • Prior Art Search & Patentability Analysis
  • Preparation of Patentability Search Reports (PSR)
  • Claims Optimisation and Recommendations
  • Drafting & Filing of Patent Applications
  • Responding to Patent Office Examinations and Objections (FER)
  • Overview of Approvals for Patent Grant
  • Patent Term, Maintenance & Renewal Procedures

Module 5: Overview of Geographical Indications, Biodiversity, and Plant Variety & Farmersâ€™ Rights

  • The Geographical Indications of Goods (Registration and Protection) Act, 1999
  • The Biological Diversity Act, 2002
