Programme Overview

This Advanced Program is designed to create job-ready professionals in business intelligence and analytics by covering the entire data pipeline — from database design and data engineering to BI solution development, visualization, and project deployment. With real-client projects, international exposure, and hands-on case work, learners gain an unmatched industry immersion into how data drives business strategy and decisions globally
