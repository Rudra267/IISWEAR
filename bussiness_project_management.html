Certificate in Business Analytics & Performance Management

This programme is tailored for aspiring leaders and working professionals aiming to build decision-making capabilities using data analytics, business intelligence, and performance management frameworks. With a strong focus on KPIs, strategic analytics, and global project exposure, the curriculum transforms learners into business-savvy analytics professionals ready for leadership roles.

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Eligibility: Any Graduate/Diploma Holder
  • Format: Online (100+ hours self-paced + live sessions)
  • Internships:International Project Exposure Included
  • EMI Options:Available

Programme Modules

Lay the foundation with essential database and project concepts.

  • Introduction to RDBMS, DBMS, and EDBMS
  • 12 Codd’s Rules of RDBMS
  • Keys, Normalization (1NF to 4NF), Entity Relationships
  • Views and Materialized Views
  • Cloud-Based RDBMS for BI Applications
  • Project: OLTP Design with Business KPIs

Learn to build data-driven strategies and measure business impact.

  • Fundamentals of Business Intelligence
  • KPIs and Strategic Performance Measures
  • Business Intelligence Frameworks
  • Role of BI in Decision Making
  • Project: BI Roadmap for a Client Use Case

Bridge the gap between raw data and actionable insights.

  • Data Modelling Techniques for BI
  • Star & Snowflake Schema Design
  • Dimensional Modelling and Fact Tables
  • Data Mart Design for Departmental Insights
  • Project: Business-Specific Data Modelling (Sales, HR, Finance)

Implement, monitor, and optimize strategic goals across departments.

  • Introduction to CPM Frameworks
  • Balanced Scorecards and OKRs
  • Financial Planning & Forecasting
  • Business Metrics Dashboards
  • Project: Cross-Functional CPM Strategy Implementation

Deliver data-driven stories using professional-grade dashboards.

  • Data Visualization Principles
  • Tools like Power BI/Tableau/Qlik
  • Report Automation and Storyboarding
  • Project: Executive Dashboard for Business Reviews

Apply your knowledge in a real-time business simulation.

  • Integrated Simulation with BI, Modelling, and CPM Concepts
  • Capstone Project: Performance Strategy for a Global Client

Leverage international internships to break into high-impact roles.

  • Work on International Projects with Global Teams
  • Interview Preparation & Strategic Role Mapping
