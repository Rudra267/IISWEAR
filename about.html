About Us

Home About Us

At IISWEAR (International institute of Software Engineering Analytics And Research), we are not just another training institute we are a career accelerator committed to producing job-ready professionals and placing them directly into our own workforce ecosystem. We are built on a bold, future-forward vision: to create a sustainable talent pipeline and eliminate external placement dependencies by offering 100% in-house job opportunities.

Headquartered in Pune, IISWEAR is dedicated to empowering graduates, freshers, and career restarters through intensive, offline classroom training that culminates in direct employment within our group companies and internal divisions. Our training is not just education it’s the first step in your career with us.

What Sets IISWEAR Apart

  • ✅ 100% In-House Placement Guarantee
    We train to hire — every eligible candidate from a job-assured program receives a confirmed job offer within our own companies.
  • ✅ Earn by Month 5
    Training is structured to fast-track you into a salaried role, with most learners earning a paycheck by the fifth month.
  • ✅ Offline, Instructor-Led Training
    We conduct in-person training in Pune with 4–5 hours of daily classroom interaction for immersive, distraction-free learning.
  • ✅ Paid Training Model
    Our fee-based structure ensures seriousness and commitment from learners and allows us to deliver uncompromised quality.
  • ✅ Tailored for Career Restarters
    Whether you’ve taken a career break or are transitioning industries, IISWEAR provides the confidence, structure, and guaranteed opportunity to re-enter the workforce.
  • ✅ No Third-Party Dependency
    We are self-reliant. We do not depend on partner companies, recruitment firms, or job portals. Your job is with us, not somewhere else.

To build a fully self-sufficient, skill-to-employment ecosystem where learning, development, and job creation happen under one roof ensuring that every trained candidate becomes a contributing employee in our own business verticals.

Career Advantage at IISWEAR

At IISWEAR, your career doesn’t begin after training it begins with it. Our exclusive in-house placement model offers learners a rare advantage: a direct path from classroom to company, with no job market uncertainty, external competition, or placement delays.

Our programs are not just job-oriented they are job-integrated. Every eligible candidate is absorbed directly into one of our group companies upon successful course completion. No third-party interviews. No external dependencies. You train with us. You work with us.

Our courses are designed backwards we start with the job role and engineer the learning experience to meet real-world applications. You’ll encounter tools, technologies, and problem-solving scenarios you’ll use in your future role.

IISWEAR is built for speed and impact. You can expect to start earning by the 5th month, with structured salary packages in roles across analytics, IT, strategy, or digital operations.

You don’t have to wait for a hiring season or placement drive. At IISWEAR, placement is a built-in feature, not an outcome.

Returning after a career break or switching fields? IISWEAR provides the structure, support, and opportunity to confidently re-enter the workforce.
