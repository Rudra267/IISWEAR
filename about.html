About Us

Home About Us

At IISWEAR (International Institute of Software Engineering Analytics and Research), we are more than just a training institute we are a career accelerator, dedicated to transforming individuals into job-ready professionals and integrating them directly into our own workforce ecosystem.Driven by a bold, future-focused vision, IISWEAR aims to build a sustainable talent pipeline and eliminate the need for external placements by offering 100% in-house job opportunities.

Headquartered in Pune, we empower graduates, freshers, and career restarters through intensive, offline classroom training that leads directly to employment within our group companies and internal divisions. At IISWEAR, training isn’t just about educationit’s the beginning of your career with us.
